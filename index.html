Personal Injury • Trial Attorney

– Integrity Means Everything –

Insurance companies fight every case. The days when insurance companies would attempt to reach fair and equitable settlements without the need to put a case in suit are long gone. The day that a client could retain a general practice attorney and get a fair resolution to a case because of their relationship with the insurance company no longer exists.

The reality of today's world requires that accident victims retain an attorney who not only specializes in personal injury cases but is also a Trial Attorney. Insurance companies have a "book" on the attorneys that represent clients in personal injury cases. If you choose an attorney who is in general practice, or an attorney who advertises as a personal injury attorney but does not try cases, you will never receive fair and equitable compensation for your injuries. The insurance companies know the experienced trial lawyer — we are few and far between.

Areas of Practice

Automobile Accident

Millions of people are injured in automobile accidents every year. You are not alone. I will guide you to the compensation you rightfully deserve, without the hassle.

Motorcycle Accident

Motorcyclists are among the most vulnerable on the road. I am dedicated to getting you the full compensation you deserve so you can get back on the road with confidence.

Slip & Falls

Property owners have a duty to keep their premises safe. Having recovered millions for victims of slip and fall accidents, I will fight to ensure you receive fair compensation.

Animal / Dog Bites

Dog bite incidents are almost always preventable. If you or a loved one has been bitten by an animal, I will guide you through the process and fight for the compensation you deserve.

Medical Malpractice

If you or a family member has been harmed due to negligence within the medical industry, do not wait to take action. I will pursue the full compensation you are owed.

Nursing Home Negligence

Residents of nursing homes who are mistreated, neglected, or abused deserve justice. I will fight aggressively to hold those responsible accountable and secure the compensation your family deserves.

Legal Malpractice

When an attorney fails to competently represent you and you suffer harm as a result, you have rights. I will hold negligent legal professionals accountable for the damage they have caused.

Worker's Compensation

If you were injured on the job, you have a right to compensation. I will cut through the complexity and ensure your employer and their insurer meet their full legal obligations.

Product Liability

Injured by a defective or falsely marketed product? Manufacturers must be held accountable. I will pursue the full compensation you deserve, quickly and efficiently.
